Excellent update.
If you can now do **unassisted pull-ups**, your training phase changes completely.

You are no longer in:

* “foundation”
* or “learning the movement”

Now you enter:

# ⭐ WEEK 12 — ATHLETIC CUT + CALISTHENICS SKILL PHASE

### Focus:

* 🔥 Faster fat loss
* 💪 Pull-up strength progression
* 🤸 Skill development
* ⚡ Athletic movement
* 🧱 Maintain joint safety

### Equipment:

* Pull-up bar
* Wooden parallettes
* Resistance band (7–15 kg)

### Strategy Shift (Important)

We now use:

* Higher movement density
* More compound skill work
* More hanging work
* More athletic conditioning
* Slightly lower rest periods

WITHOUT:

* junk fatigue
* sloppy reps
* overtraining

---

# 🔑 WEEK 12 RULES

* ❌ No failure reps
* ❌ No long random cardio
* ❌ No kipping pull-ups
* ✅ Explosive concentric, controlled eccentric
* ✅ Core every day
* ✅ 45–60 min max

---

# 🔥 DAY 1 — Pull-Up Power + Athletic Core

## 🎯 GOAL

Increase clean pull-up strength and improve visible upper-body structure.

---

## 1️⃣ WARM-UP (2 rounds)

* Dead hang → 30s
* Scapular pull-ups ×8
* Band pull-aparts ×15
* Arm circles ×20

---

## 2️⃣ MAIN WORKOUT

1. **Pull-ups** → 6 × 3
   *(full ROM, chest tall)*

2. **Negative pull-ups** → 3 × 3 (6s down)

3. **Band rows** → 3 × 15

Rest: 90s

---

## 3️⃣ SKILL WORK

* Top hold → 3 × 15s
* Active hang → 3 × 25s

---

## 4️⃣ FAT LOSS FINISHER

3 rounds:

* Mountain climbers ×30s
* High knees ×30s
* Rest ×30s

---

## 🧠 COACH’S CUE

**“Explode up. Own the descent.”**

---
